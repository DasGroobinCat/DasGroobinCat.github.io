Who is Big Jojo?

"Big Jojo" (Chinese: 大周周) is my alter ego, adopted after I went to China in 2009 to pursue a career teaching English. "Jojo" was my childhood nickname which I also used in China because it was easy for the local people to remember. My kindergarten students called me "Big Jojo."

I created the orignal logo in 2008 using Inkscape, a vector graphics program. I first used the same colors as the Google logo, but later changed them to match the color pallette used by the ColecoVision videogame console.
