Big Jojo's English Flashcards

Below is a Google Drive link to all the flashcards I produced while teaching in China. 705 cards in total, packaged in a 378 MB ZIP file. Each card is 200 DPI and formatted for printing on A4 sized paper (ISO 216).

IMPORTANT NOTICE

I tried to use public domain images when possible. In some cases, the best choice was a non-free image which was used without permission from the copywrite holder. These cards are provided for reference ONLY. Do NOT sell, distribute, or use these cards in classrooms. For private, personal use only.
